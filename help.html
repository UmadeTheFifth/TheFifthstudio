Follow these simple steps to view and download your photos and videos:

1

Check Your Email

After your session or event, you'll receive an email with your login credentials. This typically arrives within 5-7 business days.

2

Visit the Login Page

Click the Login link in the navigation menu or the button in the email we sent you.

3

Enter Your Credentials

Use the email address and password provided in your welcome email. Your credentials are unique and personal to you.

4

Explore Your Gallery

Browse your personalized gallery, view full-size images, and download your favorites. Your gallery remains accessible for 90 days.

Note: If you haven't received your login credentials within 7 business days, please check your spam folder or contact us.

‚¨áÔ∏è Downloading Your Media

Individual Downloads

  1. Click on any image to view it in full size
  2. Click the "Download" button below the image
  3. The high-resolution file will be saved to your device

Bulk Downloads

  1. Select multiple images using the checkbox on each thumbnail
  2. Click "Download Selected" at the top of the gallery
  3. Your browser will download a ZIP file containing all selected images

Download All

  1. Click the "Download All" button at the top of your gallery
  2. Confirm the download when prompted
  3. A ZIP file with all your media will be downloaded
Tip: For large galleries, we recommend using the "Download All" feature with a stable internet connection. The download may take several minutes depending on file sizes.
